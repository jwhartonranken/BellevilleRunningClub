Membership Benefits (2021)

– Race DISCOUNTS (in past years, registered members were offered more than $100 in race fee discounts).
Discount codes are sent to only registered members.
– 10% off all BRC gear/merchandise (an online code for the discount will be sent to registered members).
– 10% off purchases from Toolen’s Running Start in Shiloh (must mention in store you are a BRC member) and 10%
off purchases from the Running Warehouse (an online discount code will be sent to registered members).
– Eligibility to compete in the annual BAM (BRC Awesome Member) contest
– Voting privileges on the BRC budget and bylaws.
– Membership in the Road Runner Clubs of America (RRCA—governing body over BRC) and a complimentary online
subscription of its Club Running.
– Opportunity to participate in the BRC booth at the annual downtown Belleville Chili Cookoff (per RRCA
guidelines, only “registered” members can participant in events in which the transfer/handling of money takes
place).
– Support running/walking in Belleville. Because of our affiliation with the RRCA and efforts of club members,
Belleville was designated a Runner Friendly Community. In addition, the BRC is partnering with the Belleville
Parks and Recreation Department through an annual donation for improvements along the MetroLink trail in
Belleville. In 2017, the BRC donated trees and trash cans along the MetroLink trail.
– You become a “card-carrying member” of one of the coolest groups around!

For more information or questions, please contact brc@bellevillerunningclub.com.
